A Visual Look at Our Favorite Movies

by LUCY MAYNARD
There are movies we think are good and movies we all pay to go see. These movies are not always the same. This is a visual look at our favorite movies, using IMDB ratings and data of box office ticket sales (adjusted for inflation) from Box Office Mojo

Successful Directors

Most directors want their films to be profitable at the box office and liked by audiences. Some did. This graph shows the number of movies by director on IMDB's top ranked list and Box Office Mojo's highest adjusted grossing movies list.

Top Grossing Movies by Studio

Movie studios want their movies to be successful at the box office, but some studies have succeeded more than others. This graph shows the studios with 2 or more movies on Box Office Mojo's list of the top 200 adjusted domestic grossing movies.

#This number includes movies by Disney and Buena Vista | *MGM and United Artists merged in 1982

Top Ranked Movies by Decade

Movies are timeless. But some decades produced more fan-favorite films than others. This graph shows how many of IMDB's top 250 ranked movies came out in each decade. The green line shows the median number of top ranked films for each decade.

Star Wars by the Numbers

Star Wars has captivated the imaginations of generations of fans. But each film experienced different levels of fan acceptance and box office success. This graph compares the domestic adjusted gross income and IMDB ranking of the Star Wars films.
